üìã Project Overview

Technical Sales CRM is a comprehensive Customer Relationship Management system specifically designed for technical sales teams. It provides specialized tools for managing sales pipelines, tracking technical customer requirements, automating follow-ups, and analyzing sales performance with technical insights.

üéØ Core Features


1. Customer Management & Monitoring

Comprehensive Customer Profiles: Store detailed technical information including tech stack, current solutions, and pain points

Advanced Search & Filtering: Search by company name, contact person, tech stack, or industry

Customer Status Tracking: Prospect ‚Üí Qualified ‚Üí Active ‚Üí VIP ‚Üí Inactive lifecycle

Technical Details Storage: Track technical requirements, budget ranges, and specific challenges


2. Four-Color Sales Funnel System

Yellow Funnel: "Closable this month" - High probability deals closing soon

Pink Funnel: "Newly quoted" - Recent quotes (0-30 days old)

Green Funnel: "Project based" - Long-term project opportunities

Blue Funnel: "Services offered" - Service and support contracts


3. Lead Qualification Engine

Lead Scoring System: Automated scoring based on budget, authority, need, and timeline (BANT)

Technical Fit Assessment: Evaluate compatibility with customer's tech stack

Lead Source Tracking: Website, referral, social media, events, cold calls

Conversion Pipeline: New ‚Üí Contacted ‚Üí Qualified ‚Üí Converted


4. Intelligent Email System

Template Management: Create, upload, and manage email templates

Variable Personalization: Dynamic variables for customer name, deal details, dates

Email Categories: Proposal, follow-up, demo, quote, meeting, general

Tracking & Analytics: Open rates, click rates, and engagement metrics

WYSIWYG Editor: Built-in editor for creating professional emails


5. Dashboard & Analytics

Real-time KPIs: Deals won/lost, pipeline value, conversion rates

Funnel Visualization: Color-coded sales funnel distribution

Activity Heatmaps: Visual representation of sales activities

Performance Metrics: Individual and team performance tracking

Forecasting: Revenue predictions and trend analysis


6. Follow-up & Reminder System

Smart Follow-up Suggestions: AI-powered recommendations based on deal stage

Calendar Integration: Schedule meetings, calls, and demos

Automated Reminders: Email and system notifications for pending tasks

Priority-based Scheduling: Intelligent scheduling based on deal priority


7. Activity Tracking

Complete Activity Log: Record all customer interactions

Multi-type Activities: Calls, emails, meetings, demos, proposals

Outcome Tracking: Document results and next steps

Time-based Reporting: Analyze time spent per deal/customer


8. Reports & Analytics

Comprehensive Reporting: Win/loss analysis, lead source effectiveness

Performance Dashboards: Team and individual performance metrics

Export Options: CSV, PDF, and printable reports

Custom Time Periods: Weekly, monthly, quarterly, yearly analysis
